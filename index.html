The Story Behind Dose Diary
💜

Built for someone I love.

"My wife was diagnosed with a kidney condition that required a complex, lifelong medication regimen. I needed a simple, reliable way to make sure she never missed a dose."

Dose Diary started as a personal project — not a startup, not a product roadmap. I built it because my wife, a kidney patient, was managing multiple medications on a strict schedule that changed frequently based on her treatment progress.

Existing apps were either too simple to handle complex intervals, or too complicated to use daily. So I built exactly what she needed: a quiet, reliable companion that remembers her schedule so she doesn't have to.

Over time, the app grew to support others too — people on HRT, long-term illness survivors, anyone whose health journey requires precision and consistency. But at its heart, Dose Diary is still a love letter — to every patient and every caregiver who refuses to let illness win.

Features

Everything you need to stay on track

Designed for real patients with real complexity.

📅
Flexible Scheduling
Daily, weekly, every 2 weeks, every 3 weeks — handle any regimen your doctor prescribes, not just simple daily reminders.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously.

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Contact: jay.tran@kreebox.com

🔒 Data stays on your device

Dose Diary stores all your medication data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal health information on external servers.

Your medication history, schedules, and logs are stored only on your iPhone and are never sent to us or any third party.

📊 Analytics & Advertising

To keep the app free, we display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited, anonymized data to serve relevant ads. You will be asked for your tracking preference when you first open the app via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

  • Advertising data (ad interactions) — used to serve ads
  • Crash data — used to improve app stability
  • Product interaction — used to improve app experience

🔔 Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Dose Diary schedules local reminders on your device. These notifications are processed entirely on-device — no data is sent to our servers.

👶 Children's Privacy

Dose Diary is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at jay.tran@kreebox.com
