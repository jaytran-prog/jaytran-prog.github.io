About

Dose Diary helps you track any medication schedule — from complex hormone therapy (HRT) to everyday prescriptions. Set reminders, log doses, and never miss a treatment.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Storage

All your medication data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal health information on external servers.

2. Advertising

Dose Diary displays ads via Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show relevant ads. You can opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

3. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs. No usage data is sent to our servers.

4. Notifications

Dose Diary requests notification permission solely to send medication reminders. This data stays on your device and is never shared.
