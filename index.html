The Story Behind Dose Diary
💜

Born from personal need.
Grown into a community.

🏳️‍⚧️
My own HRT journey
I needed a reliable way to track my own hormone medications — doses, timing, and long-term history — without relying on paper or generic reminder apps.
💗
Caring for my wife
My wife is a kidney patient with a complex, ever-changing treatment regimen. Managing Tacrolimus, Mycophenolate, and multiple other medications daily demanded something smarter than memory.
🌏
Community knowledge
The HRT community in Vietnam has limited access to reliable medical information. I wanted to build a space to share knowledge — medically cited, accessible, and safe.

Dose Diary is not a startup product or a business plan. It started as a deeply personal tool — built on weekends, refined through real daily use by me and my wife.

Over time, I realized many others face the same challenges — people on HRT, chronic illness patients, caregivers managing medications for loved ones. So I opened it up, added an HRT reference library with medically-cited sources, and made it available for free.

If this app helps even one person stay consistent with their treatment — that's everything. Your health journey matters.

Features

Everything you need to stay on track

Designed for real patients with real complexity.

📅
Flexible Scheduling
Daily, weekly, every 2–4 weeks — handle any regimen your doctor prescribes, not just simple daily reminders.
✅
One-tap Logging
Mark medications as taken instantly. Track history with timestamps so you always know exactly when each dose was taken.
🔔
Smart Reminders
Customizable notifications timed to your dose schedule. Before meal, after meal — the reminder fits your routine.
📊
Adherence Analytics
See your weekly and monthly adherence at a glance. Understand your patterns and stay consistent with your treatment plan.
📚
HRT Reference Library
Comprehensive, medically-cited reference for hormone therapy medications. Dosages, timing, and clinical context from WPATH, UCSF, and Endocrine Society.
📱
Home Screen Widget
See today's pending medications at a glance — right on your home screen. No need to open the app.
🔒
100% Private
Your health data stays on your device. No account required, no cloud upload, no data sold. Your medical history belongs to you.
🌐
Bilingual — EN / VI
Full support for English and Vietnamese. Switch languages instantly in Settings.
⭐
Free Forever
Core features are completely free. No paywalls for medication tracking and reminders — because your health can't wait.
Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously.

Last updated: May 2026 · Contact: jay.tran@kreebox.com

🔒 Data stays on your device

Dose Diary stores all medication data locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal health information on external servers.

💜 Your medication history, schedules, and logs are stored only on your iPhone and are never sent to us or any third party.

📊 Analytics & Advertising

To keep the app free, we display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited, anonymized data to serve relevant ads. You will be asked for your tracking preference on first launch.

🔔 Notifications

Medication reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to our servers.
